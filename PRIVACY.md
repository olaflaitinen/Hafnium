# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

Hafnium processes data as a data processor on behalf of its tenants (controllers).

## Data Types

The platform deals with:

-   **PII**: Personally Identifiable Information (Names, addresses, IDs)
-   **Financial Data**: Transaction details, account numbers
-   **Behavioral Data**: Device fingerprints, login history

## Data Protection

-   **Encryption**: All PII is tokenized via the Vault service.
-   **Retention**: Configurable per tenant policy.
-   **Access**: Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and Just-In-Time (JIT) access logs.

## Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact privacy@hafnium.dev.
